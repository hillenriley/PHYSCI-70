### Week Five: Electronic Input Devices <p> This week we started learning about electronic input devices. The main goal of this week was learning how to get data from our circuit systems and find the minimum and maximum values for that data. In order to find the minimum and maximum values, I had to look at both the output pages and the graphs (shown in photos below) to find the highest and lowest numbers. </p> <p> After looking at all of the circuit designs, I decided to use a Tx Rx touch sensor for the first part of this assignment. This circuit was actually really easy to build in comparison to past circuits we have made in this class. All I really had to do was have one wire come into the - and another wire go into the - port, and then i connected those smaller wires to longer alligator clips. I then connected those clips to the copper paper.
